About me

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Industrial Engineering at Tecnologico de Monterrey, and an Adjunct Faculty at the Department of Food Science at the University of Arkansas, Division of Agriculture.

Currently, I am a member of the

  • Editorial board of the Journal of Quality Technology.
  • Editorial board of Quality Engineering.
  • Council of the Quality, Statistics, and Reliability section of INFORMS.
  • Sistema Nacional de Investigadoras e Investigadores, Level 1.

From 2022 to 2023, I was an Assistant Professor at the Department of Industrial Engineering at the University of Arkansas. From 2020 to 2022, I was an Assistant Adjunct Professor at the Department of Statistics at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), and a Junior Postdoctoral Fellow of the Research Foundation Flanders (FWO), Belgium. From July 2018 to September 2019, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Department of Biosystems at KU Leuven, Belgium. In May 2018, I obtained a Ph.D. in Applied Economics at the University of Antwerp, Belgium. The supervisors of my Ph.D. thesis were Prof. Peter Goos and Dr. Eric Schoen.

I was born and raised in Monterrey, Nuevo Leon, Mexico.
